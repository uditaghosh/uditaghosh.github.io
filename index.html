Udita Ghosh

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Vision and Learning Group at the University of California, Riverside, under the supervision of Dr. Amit K. Roy-Chowdhury. My PhD research focuses on developing sample-efficient algorithms for sequential decision-making agents, with particular emphasis on offline imitation learning and preference-based learning. Additionally, I explore robust uncertainty quantification methods and investigate how large foundation models can enhance planning capabilities in autonomous systems. Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked as a data scientist for three years after earning my Master's in Computer Science from the Indian Statistical Institute.
